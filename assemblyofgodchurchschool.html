Assembly Of God Church School — Proposed

This area is proposed for construction of one of the most prestigious schools in Kolkata, The Assembly Of God Church School. The area is within 5 minutes walking distance from the proposed township. This will allow residents of the township access to high quality schooling nearby.

With an area of 12 bighas, this project has sufficient area for all modern amenities within a school. Currently, this area is connected by public transport. The proposed 30 feet wide road will further improve connectivity to Basanti Highway and Kolkata Leather Complex, which is within 2 kms from the school.

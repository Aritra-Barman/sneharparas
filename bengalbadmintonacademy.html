Bengal Badminton
Academy

This is one of our most prestigous projects which we have undertaken. Bhoomi puja has been performed and foundation stone has been layed on 23 April, 2022 by Dr. Subrata Gupta, IAS. This is currently under construction and expected completion date is January 2023.

Spread over an area of 5 bighas, there is a plan to construct 3 courts initially, but it can increase in due course of time. There is also plan for a hostel inside the campus for players to stay. Other amenities in the project include a fully equipped gymnasium and swimming pool.

The motto of construction of this Bengal Badminton Academy facility is to produce national and international level badminton players. Players are expected to arrive from different states within India, as well as from Nepal and Bhutan.
